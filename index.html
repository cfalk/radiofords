The historic and acclaimed Haverford Radio Station is back! Over the past few years a few people have been working hard to bring back the near-century-old station as a platform for all the diverse interests and musical tastes of the Haverford community.

In keeping with this goal, this semester the Haverford Radio Station will begin regular DJed shows and a variety of other projects such as news reports, sports casting, and outreach to artists from the world outside of Haverford.

How can you make the Haverford Radio station your own? Were looking for DJs, journalists, sports enthusiasts, tech savvies, graphic designers, promoters, managers, and anyone else whos interested! No prior experience required! If youre interested in any of these positions, have any ideas, or are curious at all, email us!
